About this project

The Long Run is a personal essay series at the intersection of economics, machine learning, and agriculture. The analytical approach draws on systems thinking — tracing structural causes rather than surface symptoms, and holding every argument to the standard of verifiable evidence.

Each essay addresses one question with precision. No projections without labels. No data without sources. No conclusions without causal caution.
