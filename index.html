About this project

Atlas is a personal collection of visual economic essays on West Africa, built in the tradition of Edward Tufte's The Visual Display of Quantitative Information. Every chart uses verifiable primary source data — FAO, IEA, World Bank, IITA — and only what can be verified appears. Estimates and projections are always labelled as such.

The goal is not to be comprehensive, but to be precise: one question, one dataset, one clear answer per essay.
